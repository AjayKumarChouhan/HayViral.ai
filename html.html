Under the hood

What happens from idea to live post

Five stages. One continuous pipeline. Nothing re-entered by hand.

Strategy sets the direction
Audience, niche, and channels feed a generated playbook instead of a blank page.
Trend Scraping & Breakdown find the hook
Rising audio and hashtags surface, and a competitor's top video gets dissected frame-by-frame.
Scripting & Create build the asset
Claude drafts the script and voice-over, Canva renders the visual, and the canvas exports it.
Calendar assigns and queues it
The finished creative lands in its row, assigned to a teammate, status flips to ready.
Publish & Report close the loop
It goes live across platforms and the dashboard tracks clicks, conversions, and retention afterward.

The unfair advantage isn't any single feature — it's that strategy, research, creation, and reporting share the same workspace. Nothing gets re-typed, re-uploaded, or re-explained between steps.
